WINGS

The aim of Web Development Wing is to create awareness about Web Development in our college. We try to achieve this by organizing different workshops and encouraging the juniors to work on exciting projects. During the semester, our wing members work on the different websites hosted for the college and different fests. We try to improve technical culture of our college by collaborating with other technical wings for a better reach.

Our aim is to help the students passionate about android technologies turn into technically potent developers capable to take on real world challenges with the help of their skills. We conduct workshops to encourage and help juniors to step into this field, through wing projects we aim to improve collaboration and team work and helping our members learn new technologies and flex their skills.

Bunch of design enthusiasts who love to create things! All the posters, logos, brochures, apps, t-shirts, websites, videos, etc, made in IIITA are designed by us. Everything ranging from the design of official placement brochure to the t-shirt design of Fests, the small family of design Wing does it all.

The Blockchain Wing of GeekHaven is committed to promoting the adoption and development of blockchain technology in India. It believes that blockchain has the potential to revolutionize many industries, and it is working to ensure that Indian students have the skills and knowledge they need to be leaders in this field.

We improve the AI and ML culture of our college in a multitude of ways. The fundamental method is introducing students to the domains of AI and ML. During the semester, our members provide guidance to those interested in doing projects that incorporate elements of AI. Most of all, we encourage people to participate in competitions and hackathons and build AI projects to win them.

Coding is the link between human instructions and computer understanding. And here at CC wing, we aim to break the shackles of monotonous coding culture to ace various coding confrontations. Our workshops empower talented students to overcome coding challenges and interview hurdles. to be the best. Beyond campus contests, CC Wing hosts global coding competitions, providing essential exposure for success. Elevate your coding journey with us!

In the ever-evolving realm of cybersecurity, the learning journey is intense due to constant technological advancements. Navigating the dynamic cybersec landscape demands constant learning. We unite enthusiasts to share knowledge and collaborate: 1. Workshops for newcomers. 2. Sharing experiences, like CTF challenges and vulnerable systems, through insightful blogs. 3. Contributing to open-source projects. 4. Supporting beginners on Discord.

FOSS (Free and Open Source Software) Wing’s leading aim is to nurture and encourage opensource development, making students learn about open source workflow Through workshops, we guide them in using essential tools like VCS(Git) and GitHub.The Open Code event offers a platform for students to initiate their collaboration journey by merging their first pull request with an open code base. This emphasizes teamwork, organized software development, and rapid issue awareness.
